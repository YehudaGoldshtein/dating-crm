# Dating Texting Guide

## Do

- **Be genuine** — if you want to text, just text. No games.
- **Keep it light early on** — save heavy topics for in person.
- **Match their energy** — mirror response time and message length.
- **Ask open-ended questions** — reference something specific about them.
- **Be direct about intentions** — ambiguity leads to ghosting.
- **Move toward meeting** — texting is a bridge, not the destination.

## Don't

- **Be over-excited or overly nice** — too many exclamation marks, compliments, or eager energy sounds robotic and try-hard. Keep it chill and natural, like you're texting a friend you're into, not performing enthusiasm.
- **Double/triple text** unanswered messages — looks desperate.
- **Use sarcasm early** — tone doesn't translate, things get misread.
- **Send walls of text** — keep it balanced, especially early.
- **Have conflict over text** — call or talk in person.
- **Obsess over response timing** — the right person isn't keeping score.
- **Be generic** — "hey" and "wyd" on repeat is boring.

## Remember

No single "right" texting style exists. Problems come when different styles clash without awareness. The goal is always to get to a real conversation.
